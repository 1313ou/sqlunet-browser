WordNet
WordNet is a lexical database of semantic relations between words.
VerbNet
The VerbNet project maps PropBank verb types to their corresponding Levin classes. It is a lexical resource that incorporates both semantic and syntactic information.
PropBank
PropBank is a corpus that is annotated with verbal propositions and their arguments—a "proposition bank".
BNC
The British National Corpus (BNC) is a 100-million-word text corpus of samples of written and spoken English from a wide range of sources.
