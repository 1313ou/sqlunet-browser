WordNet
WordNet is a lexical database of semantic relations between words.
SyntagNet
SyntagNet is a manually-curated large-scale lexical-semantic combination database which associates pairs of concepts with pairs of co-occurring words, hence capturing sense distinctions evoked by syntagmatic relations.
BNC
The British National Corpus (BNC) is a 100-million-word text corpus of samples of written and spoken English from a wide range of sources.
