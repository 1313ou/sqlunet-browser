Direct antonym
A pair of words between which there is an opposition relation. An opposite and inherently incompatible word. Antonymy is a lexical relation, not a semantic one. Heavy and light are antonyms while ponderous and light, heavy and weightless are not (they're indirect antonyms). Two words are antonyms if their meanings are opposite in some way such as:
