Hyponym/Troponym
Subordinate (i.e. more specific). The specific term used to designate a member of a class.
A verb expressing a specific manner elaboration of another verb. Manner name (a word that denotes a manner of doing something).
X is a hyponym of Y iff
→ X is a (kind of) Y
X is a troponym of Y iff
→ to X is to Y in some manner
