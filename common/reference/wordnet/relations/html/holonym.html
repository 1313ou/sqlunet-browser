Holonym
The name of the whole of which the meronym names a part, a member or the substance. Whole name (a word that names the whole of which a given word is a part).
W/O/C is a holonym of P/S/M iff
→P is a part of W
→S is a substance of O
→M is a member of C
inverse: meronym
