Hyponym
Subordinate (i.e. more specific). The specific term used to designate a member of a class. A concept that is more specific than a given concept. A hyponym of something is its subtype.
Troponym. A verb expressing a specific manner elaboration of another verb. Manner name (a word that denotes a manner of doing something).
H is a hyponym of X iff
→H is a (kind of) X
H is a troponym of X iff
→to H is to X in some manner
inverse: hypernym
